WebGPU based Las & LAZ viewer prototype:
  • Highly experimental and crashy.
  • Requires Chrome Canary 89
  • To enable WebGPU, open this URL: 
    chrome://flags/#enable-unsafe-webgpu
  • Github: https://github.com/m-schuetz/Potree2
Instructions:
  • Drag & Drop LAS or LAZ files into the browser.
  • After 1-2 seconds, you should start seeing your point cloud in 3D
    while it's beeing loaded.
  • Try different rendering modes. The default, "compute/packed" is
    fastest on RTX 2080, but on older GPUs, "points" might be faster.
Current Limitations:
  • Limited to 150 million points!
  • No LOD! On low-end GPUs, try to stay below 50M points!
  • LAZ files limited to around 150MB!
  • No memory management!
    Reload page before loading a new data set!
