WebGPU LAS file viewer. (no LAZ, yet)
  • Highly experimental and crashy.
  • Requires the Chrome Canary 89 browser.
  • To enable WebGPU, open this URL: 
    chrome://flags/#enable-unsafe-webgpu
  • Drag & Drop one or more las files into the browser window.
  • I recommend to load at most 10M to 100M points, depending on your hardware.
    • 119 M points, 40fps on an RTX 2080
    • 145 M points, 56fps on an RTX 2080
      (Viewpoint and order matter. Sometimes more points are rendered faster.)
    • 25 M points, 144fps on an RTX 2080
  • Reload page (F5) before loading another point cloud.
  • Github: https://github.com/m-schuetz/Potree2
Rendering modes:
  • atomic: Render with compute shader
  • atomic/dilate: Like atomic, but increase size in post-process.
  • points/quads: Render as "point-list" or instanced quads, depending on size.
  • points/dilate: Render as "point-list", increase size in post-process.
